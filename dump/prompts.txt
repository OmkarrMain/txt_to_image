2. Prompt: A majestic dragon perches atop a snow-capped mountain, its scales gleaming in the sunlight. Below, a lone figure stands on a rocky outcrop, gazing up at the dragon with a mixture of fear and awe. The scene is set against a backdrop of rugged cliffs, swirling mist, and a vast, endless sky.

3. Prompt: In a bustling market square of a steampunk city, airships soar overhead while vendors sell their wares from intricately designed stalls. The scene is filled with a mix of futuristic technology and Victorian-era aesthetics.

4. Prompt: Deep underwater, a mermaid princess swims gracefully through a coral reef teeming with colorful fish and swaying sea plants. Sunlight filters through the water, creating dappled patterns of light on the sandy ocean floor. The mermaid's iridescent tail glimmers as she navigates the vibrant underwater world with a sense of wonder and curiosity.

5. Prompt: In a post-apocalyptic wasteland, a lone survivor treks across a barren landscape littered with the remnants of a once-thriving civilization. Dilapidated buildings loom in the distance, their crumbling facades a stark contrast to the harsh, desolate surroundings. The survivor is clad in tattered clothing and carries a makeshift weapon, their expression one of determination and resilience in the face of adversity.
